# Salary Analysis

## Objective
- *The objective of this project was to utilise advanced excel skills, to use the power side of excel in order to analyse job postings data and compare jobs based on median salary for jobs based in the United States and also non-US jobs*
- *Additionally analyse skills required per job on average, as well as the skill likelihood*

 ## Questions Answered
 1.👨‍💻 What are the top skills of these data jobs?  
 2.🤷‍♂️Do more skills mean more money for the job positions advertised?  
 3.💹What is the salary/pay for the top ten skills in the data science field?  
 4.🗺Is the median salary for jobs in the data science field higher in the USA or outside the USA?  


## Skills Utilized
 - Power Query`ETL`
 - Power Pivot `RELATIONSHIPS`
 - Pivot  Tables & Pivot Charts
 - Slicers
 - Power Pivot Measures
